About Webdivision

Two decades of brand craft, one creative director.

Webdivision brings together strategic thinking and creative execution to help organizations modernize their marketing, improve operational efficiency, and connect more effectively with their audiences.

Todd Blackmon, Creative Director

Every brand has a stronger version of itself. Our job is to find it, then build the strategy and creative to prove it.

Todd Blackmon, Founder & Creative Director, Webdivision

Todd Blackmon founded Webdivision to bring hands-on creative leadership to companies that need more than a vendor. They need a partner who understands both the strategy and the craft. Todd is a Creative Director with more than 20 years of experience leading multi-disciplinary teams to deliver award-winning brand strategies and marketing campaigns across digital, print, and video, with work spanning healthcare, legal, wellness, automotive, real estate, technology, and professional services.

That range is the point. Brands rarely fail from a lack of ideas. They fail when strategy and execution pull in different directions. Webdivision exists to close that gap. Past campaigns have increased client engagement by as much as 40 percent, grown web traffic by 50 percent year over year, and improved tradeshow lead capture by 25 percent, all while keeping the creative sharp and the strategy sound.
